Recipe #1

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Onions One
Rice 2 Cups
Kimchi Handful
Meat Cup, Diced
Veggie Variety
  • Carrots
  • Spinach
  • Bean Sprouts
 You decide

Step 1: Cook the eggs and scramble them but take them out just a minute befo they're fully cooked.

Step 2: Put sesame oil in the pan and add the day old rice. Add some water if the rice seems dry.

Step 3: While the rice is cooking, cut the kimchi into small peices. While at it, cut the other veggies to add in at the same time.

Step 4: Add in the kimchi peices with some of the juice and the other veggies

Step 5: Add in the eggs and finish mixing everything in

Step 6: Ready to serve!
